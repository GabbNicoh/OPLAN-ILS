Valentine's Day Giving Of Flower For Elderly
In celebration for this years Valentine's Day the Barangay has given out flowers for the elderly who are currently in Barangay 743.
Giving of School Supplies
Giving back to the community is one of the mission of Barangay 743. Last June they gave out free school supplies for the children of the community this 2019.
Earthquake Drill
Earthquake Awareness 2019. It promotes the community safety and awareness so that they are prepared in the event of an untimely disaster occurs.
Anti-Dengue Campaign
In the vision to promote safety in the community, the officials has a free anti-dengue injection to kids so that they are vaccinated and prevent future dengue sickness.
Patak Polio Campaign
Promotes the safety of the members of the community by launching the Patak Polio Campaign that aims to increase the immunity of the community to Polio

Decklogging
